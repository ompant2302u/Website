About the Author

Hi, I’m Mark Zuckerberg, the founder and CEO of Meta Platforms, formerly known as Facebook. I started Facebook in 2004 while studying at Harvard, with the goal of connecting people across the world. Over the years, we’ve grown from a simple social network to a family of apps and services, including Instagram, WhatsApp, and the virtual reality platform, Quest. My current focus is on building the metaverse—a more immersive and interconnected digital space that enables people to socialize, work, and create in entirely new ways. Technology has always been my passion, and I’m dedicated to developing tools that bring people closer together, no matter where they are in the world.
